About me

Juan Manuel received his electrical engineering degree from the National University of Comahue, Neuquén, Argentina, in 2003 and master and doctor engineering degree from the University of Seville of Spain in 2007 and 2009, respectively. Since 2004 he has been with the Department of Electrical Engineering, University of Seville, where he is currently an Associate Professor.
